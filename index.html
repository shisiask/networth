Features

Everything You Need to Manage Your Finances

A comprehensive platform designed to centralize all aspects of personal finance into one intuitive system.

Bank Account Tracking

Connect and monitor multiple bank accounts in one dashboard. Track balances, view transactions, and get a complete picture of your finances.

Budget Management

Create monthly budgets, categorize spending, and receive alerts when you're approaching limits. Stay on track with your financial goals.

Credit Score Monitoring

Track your credit score over time with historical logs. Understand what affects your score and build healthy credit habits.

Bill Reminders

Never miss a payment with automated bill reminders. Track recurring payments, due dates, and manage your payment schedule.

Investment Portfolio

Monitor your investments and track portfolio performance. See your asset allocation and growth over time.

Savings Goals

Set and track savings goals for emergencies, travel, or major purchases. Visualize your progress and stay motivated.

Credit Card Management

Track credit card transactions, minimum payments, and utilization. Keep your credit card spending in check.

Receipt Upload

Upload and store digital receipts for organization and audit purposes. Keep all your transaction records in one place.

Income Tracking

Log all income sources including jobs, freelance work, and financial aid. Understand your cash flow patterns.

Your Security is Our Priority

We take security seriously. Our platform includes comprehensive tracking and protection features to keep your financial data safe.

Session Tracking Detailed session logs provide visibility into all user activity and login patterns.
Login Attempt Logs Monitor and detect potential unauthorized access attempts to your account.
Data Integrity SQL-based architecture ensures data integrity and efficient querying across all operations.
Automated Alerts Receive notifications for suspicious activity, unusual spending, and important updates.
